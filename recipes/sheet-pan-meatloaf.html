sheet Pan Meatload

Description

This meatloaf cooks in about 1 hour and has minimal cleanup required. It's perfect for a weeknight meal. Pair with three-ingredient macaroni and cheese, and a vegetable to round out this meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 libra de carne molida
  • 2 huevos grandes
  • ¾ taza de pan rallado panko
  • ¼ de taza de cebolla finamente picada
  • ¼ de taza de salsa BBQ, cantidad dividida
  • 1 cucharada de salsa inglesa
  • 1 cucharada de ajo en polvo
  • sal y pimienta negra recién molida al gusto
  • Spray para cocinar

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Combine ground beef, eggs, bread crumbs, onion, 2 tablespoons BBQ sauce, Worcestershire sauce, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in large bowl and mix by hand.
  3. Line a sheet pan with foil and spray with cooking spray. Shape meatloaf mixture evenly into a rectangular shape.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 45 minutes. Brush with remaining BBQ sauce (or more as needed) and bake until internal temperature reaches 165 degrees C (73 degrees C), about 15 more minutes.
  5. Slice meatloaf and serve with extra BBQ sauce if desired.
